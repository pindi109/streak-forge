Frequently Asked Questions

Streaks in StreakForge are calculated based on your local device time zone. If you logged your habit after midnight but before the day reset in your settings, it may have counted toward the following day. We recommend checking your timezone setting under Profile → Settings → Time Zone. Also ensure your habit's daily deadline hasn't been set too early. If you believe it's a bug, please contact us with a screenshot of your activity log and we'll investigate promptly.
Setting up reminders is simple. Open the habit you'd like a reminder for, tap Edit, then select Reminders. You can choose one or multiple daily times, pick specific days of the week, and even write a custom motivational message that will appear in your notification. Make sure StreakForge has notification permissions enabled in your phone's system settings — otherwise reminders won't fire. If they're still not arriving, try toggling the permission off and on again, then restart the app.

You can choose from preset ranges (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, this year) or set a fully custom start and end date. Charts are available in bar, line, and heatmap formats — swipe left on the chart to cycle between them. Exporting charts as images is available on the Pro plan.
