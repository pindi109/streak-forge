About StreakForge

StreakForge is a personal habit-tracking application designed to help users build and maintain positive daily habits through streak tracking, customisable reminders, progress visualisations, and motivational insights.

Key features of StreakForge include:

  • Daily habit tracking with streak counters to maintain and celebrate consistency
  • Customisable push notification reminders to help you stay on track with your goals
  • Visual progress charts and analytics providing insight into your habit history
  • Goal-setting tools and personalised habit templates to accelerate your routine
  • Achievement milestones and streak milestone celebrations

StreakForge is intended for personal use only as a motivational and organisational productivity tool. It is not a medical device, therapeutic tool, or substitute for professional health, mental health, or fitness advice.
